Travel Support

    Requests for travel support should be directed to oxfordmamls@gmail.com in advance of the meeting with an estimate of the expenses involved (travel/lodging/subsistence). Such requests will be handled on a case-by-case basis within the limits of the budget. While graduate students, young researchers, female mathematicians and members of under-represented groups are particularly encouraged to apply for such support, it should be stressed that any participants without their own sources of funding are eligible to apply.
